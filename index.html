Connor Greer is a writer from Rochester, NY. He is 30 years old, about 73 inches tall, and weighs around 178 pounds. His work is forthcoming in Boulevard and Bennington Review and has been supported by fellowships from the Mitchell Center for the Arts at the University of Houston and the Fine Arts Work Center in Provincetown, MA. In 2023, he was a finalist for the BOMB Fiction Contest. He has been in residence at Tenjinyama Art Studio in Sapporo, Japan, Dar Meso in Tunis, Tunisia, and NES Listamiðstöð in Skagaströnd, Iceland. He received an MFA in fiction from the University of Michigan. He can be reached at connoragreer [at] proton.me.
